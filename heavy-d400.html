DIESEL SILVER D400 CF-4 High Performance Heavy Duty Diesel Engine Oil

GULFLUBE DIESEL SILVER D400 ENGINE OIL SAE 20W-50 API CF-4/SG, are premium high-performance multi-grade engine oils formulated from high quality Group-ii base oils combined with CHEVRON performance additives to help provide long engine life and providing wide temperature range engine protection. The advanced product technology offers a lo ng drain interval of 5,000 Km to 7,000 Km, and the formulation that helps maximize engine durability

APPLICATIONS

GULFLUBE DIESEL SILVER D400 ENGINE OIL SAE 20W-50 API CF-4/SG, is recommended for use in all naturally aspirated and turbo-charged diesel engines fitted in trucks, tractors, buses, goods carriers (LCVs), 3-wheelers and generators, on-highway light and heavy-duty trucking, off-highway industries including: construction, mining, quarrying, and agriculture & mixed fleet applications.

Properties

GULFLUBE DIESEL SILVER D400 ENGINE OIL SAE 20W-50 API CF-4/SG, provide exclusive combinations of the latest high performance additives i.e. Chevron Oronite ensuring that the oil adapts and protects under the full range of pressures & temperatures found in modern engines – from the high temperature in the pistons, to the extreme loads found in the valve-trains. Extra active additives control & sweep away harmful soot & particles found in high performance engines.
